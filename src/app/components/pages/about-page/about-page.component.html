About - Get to know me

I am a passionate Front-End Software Developer with over 3 years of experience building modern, responsive websites and web based applications.

My journey to becoming a Software Developer began in 2017 when I moved to the United States to study Computer Science at Emporia State University (ESU). Here, I completed my undergraduate study and also spent 2 years working as a Student Web Developer for ESU's IT Department.


After graduating in 2021, I returned to my home country of New Zealand to begin my first full time position at the University of Canterbury (UC) as a Software Developer. Here, I am a member of the team responsible for the development of UC's web-based Student Management System. I have primiarly focussed on development of the frontend via Angular, although I also have experience developing our backend leveraging Django, OutSystems and NodeJS.
