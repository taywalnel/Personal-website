My journey began in 2017 when I moved to the United States to study Computer Science at Emporia State University.

Here, I completed my undergraduate study and spent my final 2 years working as a Student Web Developer for ESU's IT Department. During this time, I was fortunate to play a part in some key projects including the redevelopment of the main university website and its subsidiaries as well as supporting ESU’s rapid shift to an increased reliance on online tools brought on by COVID-19.


After graduating in 2021, I returned to New Zealand and accepted a Software Development position at the University of Canterbury.

Here, I have contributed to the development of UC's Student Management System consisting of three web-based applications leveraging Angular, Django and NodeJS. After several career milestones including acting as the lead UI developer for a period following the departure some key personal, I was promoted to the position of Associate Developer towards the end of 2022.
